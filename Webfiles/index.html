About Us

Someblogagain is a project I made for fun to get better with my web development skills, someblogagain strives for full anonymity on posts and users we aim for this goal by not saving anything what could be unique to your device or network. Someblogagain aims for you to be able to document things in your life without having to worry about the hassle of privacy and cyber criminals.
